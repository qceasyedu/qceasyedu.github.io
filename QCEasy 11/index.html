Tackling the QCE Syllabus

I'll let you in on my secret: Year 11 is hard. The jump between Year 10 and Year 11 will be tough because it's the first time you step foot into senior high school. The longer assignments, harder exams and larger amount of workload means that time management is essential. For the first time in your entire life, you must pass to obtain QCE points. And if you're anything like me, you will be stressed, but that is just your body's way of adapting. Push through, time manage, and you'll be all good! The fact that you've chosen to visit my website demonstrates your commitment to empowering yourself with knowledge. Thank you for your trust, and I hope this resource helps you excel in your studies.

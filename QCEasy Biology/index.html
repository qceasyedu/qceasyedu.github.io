Tackling the QCE Syllabus: Biology

As a recent graduate, I can relate to the immense pressure you all may feel this year. But, it will be okay! Biology requires memorisation but more so knowing what to write in assignments and your external exam answers. When you eventually start doing practice exams, you'll find that what the markers want is not always what you wrote. And it sucks, I know! So, make sure you address exactly what the question is asking (no sidetracks). The fact that you've chosen to visit my website demonstrates your commitment to empowering yourself with knowledge. Thank you for your trust, and I hope this resource helps you excel in your biology studies.
